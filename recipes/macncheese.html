Broccoli and Pancetta Mac n Cheese

Description

A comforting dish, perfect for all times of year. A simple yet delicious and filling meal that will please all!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta - 500g
  • Cheddar cheese - 300g
  • Butter - 50g
  • Broccoli - One whole, chopped finely
  • Pancetta - 100g
  • Plain flour - 25g
  • Milk - 100ml
  • Garlic powder - 2 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Fill large saucepan with boiling water and season with salt, bring to a boil and add pasta
  2. Fry Pancetta in a frying pan and stir intermittently
  3. To make a cheese sauce, melt butter in a saucepan on a low heat and season with pepper
  4. add two tbsp of plain flour, one tbsp and a time and cook the flour our for a minute
  5. As the flour cooks out, add the broccoli and garlic powder to the pancetta and mix it all together
  6. back to the saucepan, add the milk slowly and whisk to make a consistent sauce with no lumps
  7. Begin to slowly add the cheese and continue to whisk, then season the sauce to taste
  8. Sieve the pasta and combine the contents of the frying pan and two saucepans, in the large one and mix thoroughly
  9. We should be left with a tasty mixture of cheesey pasta, enjoy!
